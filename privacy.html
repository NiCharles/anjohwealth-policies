Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026
Last Updated: April 21, 2026

ANJOHWEALTH GROUP LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our group contribution tracking platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for our service, we collect:

  • Your name – to identify you within your group
  • Phone number – to send you WhatsApp messages about group contributions
  • Email address (optional) – for account recovery and notifications
  • Contribution records – to track payments and generate ledgers

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you:

  • Create an account or join a group through our web form
  • Opt in to receive WhatsApp messages (by checking the consent checkbox)
  • Send screenshots of payment receipts to our WhatsApp number
  • Interact with our service

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Send you payment reminders and contribution confirmations
  • Provide meeting announcements and group updates
  • Generate personal and group ledgers
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Improve our service

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only:

  • Within your group: Group leaders and treasurers can see member names and contribution status (but not your full phone number)
  • With service providers: We use Twilio to send WhatsApp messages. Twilio's privacy policy applies to their handling of message data.
  • When required by law: To comply with legal obligations

5. Message Frequency and Opt-Out

Message frequency varies based on your group's activity. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any WhatsApp message you receive from us. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your contribution records are stored in encrypted databases. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as you remain an active member of a group. If you leave a group or opt out, we delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of WhatsApp messages at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@anjohwealth.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
